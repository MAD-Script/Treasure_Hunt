Treasure Hunt is a puzzle and hidden object game.
There are total 50 locations, 10 locations are selected at random at the start of the game. 
Each location has a unique key code, and players must find the key code to unlock the next location.
Players must solve the riddle to get the location of the key.
Players will then insert the key to unlock the next Riddle. 
The player to complete and find all the keys wins.
